Baked Potatoes

Description

These baked potatoes are delightfully crispy on the outside, with a fluffy and tender interior that melts in your mouth. Savory notes of olive oil, salt, and pepper complement the earthy taste of the potato, while toppings like butter, sour cream, cheddar cheese, and fresh chives add a deliciously rich and tangy twist. The combined flavors create a comforting and satisfying dish, making these baked potatoes the perfect hearty side or meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 large potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 4 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 cup of sour cream
  • 1 cup of shredded cheddar
  • fresh chives

Steps of Preparation

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C).
  2. While the oven is heating, clean your potatoes thoroughly under cold water. Use a brush if necessary to remove any dirt, then pat them dry.
  3. Prick each potato several times with a fork. This helps steam escape and prevents the potatoes from bursting in the oven.
  4. Rub the potatoes with olive oil, then season them with salt and pepper. Make sure they're fully covered.
  5. Place the potatoes directly on the oven rack or on a baking sheet. Bake for about 45-60 minutes, or until the skin is crispy and the insides feel soft when probed with a fork.
  6. Remove the potatoes from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes.
  7. Once they're cool enough to handle, cut a small slit in the top of each potato. Be careful not to cut all the way through.
  8. Using a fork, fluff the inside of each potato. The goal is to break up the flesh and make it easier to mix with the toppings.
  9. Top each potato with a tablespoon of butter and let it melt into the warm potato.
  10. Add a quarter cup of sour cream and a quarter cup of shredded cheddar cheese to each potato.
  11. Garnish with chopped chives, then serve while they're still warm. Enjoy your delicious baked potatoes!
